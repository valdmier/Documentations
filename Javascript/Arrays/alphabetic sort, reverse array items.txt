how to sort items from A - Z(alphabetic):

sort:

var myArray = ["idk", "bonn bon", "zyad", "idk",
 "nobody"];

myArray.sort();

console.log(myArray);

the result:
  //b          //i          //n       //z
["bonn bon", "idk", "idk", "nobody", "zyad"]

===

how to reverse items Arranging:



var myArray = ["idk", "bonn bon", "zyad", "idk",
 "nobody"];

myArray.reverse();

console.log(myArray);


the result:

["nobody", "idk", "zyad", "bonn bon", "idk"]
