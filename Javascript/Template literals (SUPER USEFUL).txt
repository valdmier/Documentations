
Template literals (Template strings):

simply that thing allows you to do mathematical opreations in such an easy way.

---
syntax:

`string text ${expression} string text`

note: use this > ` not this' or this " بتلاقيها بنفس مكان حرف ذ

===

example:

var mangoPrice = 3,
    mangoQuantity = 32;


console.log(`You have bought ${mangoQuantity} and the price iss ${mangoPrice * mangoQuantity}SR`)

-
output: You have bought 32 and the price iss 96SR
