Cards:


A card is a flexible and extensible content container.It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

simple example:


<div class="card" style="width: 18rem;">      
<div class="card-body">            
<h5 class="card-title">Card title</h5>         
<h6 class="card-subtitle mb-2 text-muted">Card subtitle</h6>
<p class="card-text">
Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>

            <a href="#" class="card-link">Card link</a>
            <a href="#" class="card-link">Another link</a>
          </div>
        </div>
===


to give the card a header:

note: put the header before the .card-body div.

example:


            <div class="card mb-3" style="width: 250px;">
              <div class="card-header">hi I'm header</div>
            <div class="card-body">
              <h5 class="card-title">Card title</h5>
              <h6 class="card-subtitle mb-2 text-muted">Card subtitle</h6>
              <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
              <a href="#" class="card-link">Card link</a>
              <a href="#" class="card-link">Another link</a>
            </div>
          </div>

===
to give the card a footer:


note: put the footer after the .card-body div.

 <div class="col-4">
            <div class="card mb-3" style="width: 250px;">
            <div class="card-body">
              <h5 class="card-title">Card title</h5>
              <h6 class="card-subtitle mb-2 text-muted">Card subtitle</h6>
              <p class="card-text">Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
              <a href="#" class="card-link">Card link</a>
              <a href="#" class="card-link">Another link</a>
            </div>
            <div class="card-footer">hi I'm footer</div>
          </div>
        </div>
