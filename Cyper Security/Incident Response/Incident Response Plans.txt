Incident Response Plans

===
Elements of a security plan: 

- Policies

- Standards

- Procedures

===
Incident response plan:

A document that outlines the procedures to take in each step of the incident response.

===
Elements of an incidents plan

- Incident response procedures.

- System Information:

Things like network diagrams, data flow diagrams, logging, and asset inventory information.

- Other documents:

 like contact lists, forms, and templates.

==
Incident processes and procedures should be subject to regular review and testing.
