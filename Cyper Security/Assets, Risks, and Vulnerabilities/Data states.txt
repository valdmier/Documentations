Data states

===
Data:

to information translated, processed, or stored by computers. In today's connected world, data is exchanged among billions of devices linked to the internet.

===
States of Data:

- Data in Use: Data being accessed by one or more users, such as checking emails.

- Data in Transit: Data traveling from one point to another, for instance, sending a message or email while connected to the internet.

- Data at Rest: Data not currently being accessed and typically stored on a physical device, like when a laptop is closed and idle.

===
