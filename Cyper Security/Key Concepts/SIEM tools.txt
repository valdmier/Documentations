SIEM is an:
application that collects and analyzes log data to monitor critical activities in an organization. It provides real-time visibility, event monitoring and analysis, and automated alerts.

===
What do siem tools do for?
- collecting
- analyzing
- monitoring
===

S - Security 
I- information and 
E- event 
M- management

 SIEM for short, is a solution that helps organizations detect, analyze, and respond to security threats before they harm business operations.

===
Metrics:
In addition to providing a comprehensive summary of security-related data, SIEM dashboards also provide stakeholders with different metrics. Metrics are key technical attributes such as response time, availability, and failure rate, which are used to assess the performance of a software application.
===
Tpes of SIEM tools:

- Self-hosted:
ideal when an organization is required to maintain physical control over confidential data.

- Cloud-hosted SIEM:

 Cloud-hosted SIEM tools are ideal for organizations that don't want to invest in creating and maintaining their own infrastructure. starting at and follow 
Or, an organization can choose to use a combination of both self-hosted and cloud-hosted SIEM tools, known as a hybrid solution. Organizations might choose a hybrid SIEM solution to leverage the benefits of the cloud while also maintaining physical control over confidential data.
