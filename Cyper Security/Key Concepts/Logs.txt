Logs:

a record of events that occur within an organization's systems and networks.

===
An example of logs is the event viewer app in windows.

===
Types of logs:

- Firewall logs

- Network logs

- Server Logs


===
A firewall log is: (NETWORK < INTERNET)
 a record of attempted or established connections for incoming traffic from the internet. I t also includes outbound requests to the internet from within the network.

 

===
A Network Log is a: (NETWORK < DEVICES)
record of all computers and devices that enter and leave the network. It also records connections between devices and services on the network.


===
A server log is a: (INFO > INTERNET)
record of events related to services , such as websites, emails, or files shares. it includes actions such as login, password, and username request.
