 identity and access management (IAM):

focused on access and authorization to keep data secure by making sure users follow established policies to control and manage assets. 

Components of IAM:

- Identification:
providing a username, access card, or biometric data.

- Authentication:
the verification proccess to prove a person's identity.

- Authorization: 
Level of access depending on their role in the organization.

- Accountibility:
Monitoring and Recording user actions, like login attempts. To prove dta is used properly.
