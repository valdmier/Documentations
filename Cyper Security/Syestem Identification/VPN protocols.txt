- WireGuard VPN:

Speed and Simplicity: WireGuard is a newer, high-speed VPN protocol that's easy to set up. It's great for both site-to-site and individual connections. Its efficiency comes from streamlined code and it's open source for easy deployment and debugging.

Best for Speed: WireGuard shines when you need fast download speeds, like streaming videos or downloading large files.

- IPSec VPN:

Security and Compatibility: IPSec is an older and more complex VPN protocol. Many operating systems support it, making it widely adopted. It's trusted due to extensive security testing.

Preference Factors: Some prefer IPSec for its history and widespread use, while others lean toward WireGuard for potential better performance and simpler setup.
